How it went

Concept

We had 30 minutes to comme-up with a concept idea.

I used everything up until the last minute to come up with the smallest scope gameplay idea I could.

In the end I drew on big paper everything I needed to explain the game in 10 seconds and pitched it.

My work

I wanted the game to have a feel of party game that focus on interaction betweent he two players.

I designed it so players needs to comunicate toogether before and durring each levels.

I also helped the programmer when he got stuck and made the shader for the travelators.

Gameplay

Two players must manage a signle avatar to cross a dangerous area back an forth, ignite bombs and escape.

Rules

Two player control the movement of a signle avatar using their own left stick, from a top down view.

Their avatar always unravels a fuse behind him. Both player must coordinate to reach the other side of an unstable travelator field, and connect this fuse to a bomb.

Once the bomb is connected, the fuse ignites, being consumed until it reachs the bomb and trigger it's explosion. If the players didn't managed to comme back before the explosion, they lose.

The Fuse

The uraveled fuse must be long enough so that they can escape in time, forsing the players to stay some time on the dangerous travelator field.

However corssing the fuse will make the flame propagate in every direction, reducing the effective time that it will take for the bomb to explode.

Playes must optimize the length and layout of the fuse so they both have enough time to escape the explosion and enough space not to corss the fuse when they comme back.
