Exaucer is a 2D platformer using angry birds like controls to make units climb a procedurally generated tree.
Duration

3 months (2014)

Team

4 people

My roles

Game Designer
Programmer
Art Director

Context

School project

Exaucer is a sandbox experience where no goals are fixed by the game.

As a school project the game had to respect the folowing theme :

« Escalade » « Escalation »
Climbing

The player can make units climb branches by using a slingshot like input. (Similar to bird throwing in Angry Birds.)

Once thrown, units are then cached by cotton holds scattered onto the tree.

Tree generation

The “level” grows as the player climbs onto the tree.

New branches, full of climbing holds nodes, are generated depending on which direction the player is taking.

Units behaviour

When jumping from a node to another, units unwind a cotton string, linking both nodes.

Other units will follow the string to reach their siblings, making their small bells ring throughout the trip.
