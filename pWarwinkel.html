Gameplay

The game oposes two players trough the conquest of the board.

Rules

Each turn, the player throw two dices.

He can use the value of one dice of his choice to indicate the amount of units he can deposit on the board this turn, the remaining dice’s value indicate where he’s able to place those pawns.

Depending of how the player organise his pawn placement, he earns conquest points. The player that scores the most by the end of the game wins.

Design

The chaos brought by randomness is awnsered by player choice, giving him the power to organise and optimise.

Multiple play styles are available to players, some more attractive than other depending on the current situation.

This way, the tactical skill of players has a huge impact on each games.
