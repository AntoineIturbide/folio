Ultimate Bulwark is a Space Invader inspired game where you need to protect civilians from alien bombardments.
Platform

PC

Tool used

Unity3D

Duration

2 months (2014)

Team size

Solo project

My roles

Game Designer
Programmer

Context

School Project

As a school project the game had to take Space Invader and change some mechanics to make a new game.
I tryed to change the dinamic of the game by translating the focus of the player onto the projectiles.

Rules

Civilians are at the bottom of the screen, running for their lives under the chokepoint.

Bombs send by the aliens that pass through your defences will explode on the ground murdering civilians.

You’re their ultimate bulwark

The player’s primary objective is to destroy the bombs send by the aliens before they reach the ground.

Shields

Classic Space Invaders shields have moved behind the player’s area of action.

The player can anticipate which bomb will be stopped by the shields and which won't.

This allows him to gauge bombs threat level and take priority based decisions.

Explosions

Bombshell destroyed airborne will create explosions.

Explosions will damage the player and civilians if they are in range.

They will also destroy surrounding bombshell making chain reaction possible with a single player shot.
