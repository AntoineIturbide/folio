Ultimate Bulwark is a Space Invader inspired game where you need to protect civilians from alien bombardments.
Platform

PC

Tool used

Unity3D

Duration

2 months (2014)

Team size

Solo project

My roles

Programmer

Context

We chose a game originated from antic rome :

« Ludus duodecim scriptorum »
Rules

Civilians are at the bottom of the screen, running for their lives under the chokepoint.

Bombs send by the aliens that pass through your defences will explode on the ground murdering civilians.

You’re their ultimate bulwark

The player’s primary objective is to destroy the bombs send by the aliens before they reach the ground.

Multiple play styles are available to players, some more attractive than other depending on the current situation.

This way, the tactical skill of players has a huge impact on each games.
